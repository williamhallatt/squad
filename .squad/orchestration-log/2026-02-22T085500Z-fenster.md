### Convention Established
- **Tracer name:** `trace.getTracer('squad-sdk')`
- **Span naming:** `squad.{module}.{method}`
- **Attributes:** Descriptive keys like `agent.name`, `routing.tier`, `target.agents`, `spawn.mode`
- **Error handling:** `span.setStatus()` + `span.recordException()` in catch, always `span.end()` in finally
- **Import only:** `@opentelemetry/api` (never SDK packages directly)
